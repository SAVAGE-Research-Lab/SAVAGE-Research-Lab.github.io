Dr. Qian Zhou is an Associate Professor, Master Supervisor, and the Director of
the Institute of Applied Technology for System Security and Availability Engineering (SAVAGE) at Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications (NJUPT). 

With over 20 years of dedication to cyber security, Dr. Zhou leads the SAVAGE Research Lab, where her research is driven by the mission to build "Trustworthy and Resilient Intelligent Infrastructures." Her expertise spans four pillars of modern information security:

System Security: Focusing on the robustness of hardware-software co-design and the protection of critical systems against sophisticated attacks.

Intelligent System Available: Integrating AI with dependability engineering to ensure high availability in dynamic environments.

Applied Cryptographic Algorithm Design: Bridging the gap between mathematical theory and engineering implementation, with specialized experience in optimizing national cryptographic standards (e.g., SM series) for resource-constrained platforms.

Network Security and Privacy Preservation: Developing next-generation privacy-preserving frameworks, semantic-aware encrypted searches, and secure data flow protocols for the digital economy.

As a Principal Investigator (PI), Dr. Zhou has successfully led multiple high-stakes research initiatives, including the National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC) and National-level Major Security Demonstration Projects . She has also been a key contributor to China’s strategic "863" and "973" programs.

Dr. Zhou is a recognized leader in the international academic community, currently serving as an Associate Editor for Health Information Science and Systems (Springer). She has served as General Co-Chair for WISE 2024 & 2025 and Joint Chair for AP-Web 2024. She is a Senior Member of the China Computer Federation (CCF) and an executive member of various professional security committees.

Under her mentorship, her student team, "Eclipse Savage Wings," recently secured the National Grand Prize at the 14th "China Software Cup." Dr. Zhou is also a passionate educator, recipient of provincial awards for her bilingual teaching in Cyber Security Technology.
